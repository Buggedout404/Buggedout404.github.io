Inspiring Young Makers

At BuggedOut404, we ignite curiosity and empower the next generation of innovators. Through our hands-on programs, students learn to design, build, and troubleshoot real‐world electronics projects. By organizing youth‐focused hackathons, preparing custom Arduino kits, and guiding learners step by step, we cultivate problem‐solvers who:

Build Confidence: Tackling open‐ended challenges helps kids (ages 10–18) develop resilience when prototypes fail and celebrate each breakthrough. Spark Creativity: From coding simple sensors to designing interactive gadgets, students explore endless possibilities in STEM. Develop Technical Skills: Our structured curriculum teaches essential electronics, programming, and circuit‐design concepts—preparing young makers for advanced coursework or future tech careers. Whether your child is curious about robotics, eager to explore coding, or keen to join a community of like‐minded peers, BuggedOut404 provides the resources and mentorship they need. Join us, and watch your student transform ideas into working prototypes—one code loop and circuit board at a time.

Custom Arduino Kits

Handpicked modules: microcontrollers, sensors, breadboards, and wiring—everything a young builder needs. Pre‐assembled “Starter Packs” for absolute beginners, plus “Advanced Packs” for intermediate coders. All kits include step‐by‐step manuals and online video tutorials that explain circuit assembly, coding logic, and troubleshooting tips.
