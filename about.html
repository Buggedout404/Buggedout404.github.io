about buggedout404

“To nurture the next generation of makers—whether they’re 10 or 20—through hands-on electronics and collaborative challenges.” At BuggedOut404, we believe that active learning—building, testing, and iterating—is the most effective way to foster confidence, creativity, and technical skill. Our programs empower young minds to tackle open-ended problems, develop resilience when things fail, and celebrate each breakthrough as a step toward lifelong innovation.

our story

Founded by a team of educators, engineers, and curriculum designers, BuggedOut404 grew from a simple idea: give students the tools, guidance, and space to make things that move, think, and solve problems. What began as weekend workshops in a college lab has blossomed into nationwide kit shipments, virtual support communities, and flagship hackathons that draw hundreds of participants. Along the way, our alumni have gone on to win science fairs, publish research, and launch their own startups—proof that a spark of curiosity can ignite a lifetime of possibility.
